ABOUT SUKANYA

Sukanya is one of the India's leading NGO working for girl's education and empowerment.
Our goal is to make every girl child educate because girls education equally matters for the society.
We provide the free education up to intermediate and various scolarships for further education.
We have been a community of more than 5000 people so be a part of our family and help our girls to grow.
